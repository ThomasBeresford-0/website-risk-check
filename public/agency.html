Website Risk Check for Agencies

This is a neutral, one-time website compliance snapshot agencies use before launches, audits, and client handovers.

It does not promise compliance. It does not monitor anything. It simply produces a clear, timestamped record of what was detectable on a client website at a specific moment in time.

Why agencies use this

  • Creates neutral evidence before or after launches
  • Reduces subjective compliance discussions with clients
  • Provides a timestamped record for internal files
  • Can be shared without explanations or logins
  • Feels professional, boring, and defensible

Common agency use cases

  • Pre-launch website checks
  • Client onboarding audits
  • End-of-project handovers
  • Risk documentation for retained clients
  • Internal QA sign-off
This is not a SaaS platform.
There are no accounts, subscriptions, dashboards, or ongoing monitoring. Each scan is a one-off snapshot you can attach to client records or emails.

What agencies receive

  • Low / Medium / High risk summary
  • Clear checklist of detectable signals
  • Plain-English explanations
  • Timestamped, shareable PDF report
  • Permanent link suitable for client delivery

What this is not

  • Not legal advice
  • Not a GDPR certification
  • Not ongoing compliance monitoring
  • Not a cookie banner or policy generator

Website Risk Check provides a factual, point-in-time diagnostic snapshot based on detectable signals only. No compliance guarantees are made.
