FAQ

No hype. Just clear answers.

Is this legal advice or certification?

No. WebsiteRiskCheck provides a point-in-time snapshot of objectively detectable signals. It’s designed for documentation and stakeholder clarity, not legal conclusions.

What does “verifiable” mean?

The report includes an integrity hash derived from objective audit fields. The public verification page recomputes and confirms the hash, so tampering is detectable.

Do you monitor my site over time?

No. This is deliberately not a dashboard or subscription. It creates a single, timestamped artifact.

What does the preview include?

A structured summary of detectable signals: policy presence, consent indicators, tracking scripts, forms/data capture heuristics, accessibility red flags, and contact signals.

Why is it £99?

You’re paying for an audit-grade artifact: deterministic capture, immutable report generation, permanent share link, and public verification — not SaaS access.

Can I share the report with others?

Yes. Each report includes a permanent /r link and a public verification page so third parties can independently validate integrity.
