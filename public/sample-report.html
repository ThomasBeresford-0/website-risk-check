Observed signals

Risk context

Under UK GDPR and PECR, non-essential cookies and similar identifiers generally require prior consent before activation, alongside clear disclosure of purpose and vendors.

Recommended next steps
  1. Implement a consent management platform (CMP) with opt-in controls.
  2. Ensure non-essential scripts activate only after consent.
  3. Publish an accessible cookie policy detailing categories and vendors.
