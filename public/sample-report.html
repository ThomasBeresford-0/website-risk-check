Methodology

Website Risk Check performs a scope-locked crawl of public, unauthenticated pages including homepage and standard policy paths. It aggregates observable compliance and risk signals, then generates a point-in-time PDF snapshot. The integrity hash is derived from objective report data and stored server-side.
