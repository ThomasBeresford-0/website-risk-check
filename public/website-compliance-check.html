Website compliance check

This tool provides a fast, neutral way to check your website for detectable compliance and risk signals.

It does not promise compliance. It does not monitor anything. It simply shows what is visibly present on your website at a specific moment in time.

What this website compliance check looks for

  • Privacy policy and terms page presence
  • Cookie usage and consent banner detection
  • Tracking and analytics scripts
  • Forms collecting personal data
  • Basic accessibility red flags
  • Contact and business information visibility

What you get

  • A clear risk level (Low / Medium / High)
  • A checklist of detectable findings
  • Plain-English explanations
  • A timestamped, downloadable PDF report
This is a one-time snapshot.
There are no accounts, subscriptions, dashboards, or follow-up emails.

Who this is for

  • Small business owners
  • Founders with live websites
  • Agencies reviewing client sites
  • Freelancers and consultants

What this is not

  • Not legal advice
  • Not a GDPR certification
  • Not ongoing compliance monitoring
  • Not a cookie banner or policy generator

Website Risk Check provides a factual, point-in-time diagnostic snapshot based on detectable signals only.
