What this website compliance check looks for

Website Risk Check scans a small, scope-limited set of pages and records what is objectively detectable at that moment. It’s designed for fast documentation — not deep legal interpretation.

Policies & disclosures
Presence of privacy policy, terms, cookies pages and related disclosures.
Cookies & tracking signals
Common trackers, third-party scripts and analytics indicators (heuristic).
Consent indicators
Signals suggesting cookie/consent banner behaviour (heuristic).
Forms & data capture
Obvious form fields that may collect personal data (heuristic).
Accessibility notes
Basic red flags (e.g., missing alt text signals) where detectable.
Contact & identity cues
Visible contact details and business information signals on scanned pages.
